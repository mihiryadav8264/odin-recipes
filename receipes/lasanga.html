Lasanga Page

Description

Layers of wide pasta noodles, rich meat sauce, and a creamy blend of three cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles,
  • 1 lb ground beef,
  • 1 jar marinara sauce,
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese,
  • 1 egg (to bind),
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella,
  • ½ cup parmesan,
  • Italian herbs.

Steps

  1. The Sauce: Brown the beef in a skillet, drain fat, and stir in the marinara sauce.
  2. The Cheese: In a small bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and a pinch of herbs.
  3. Layering: In a baking dish, spread a little sauce. Layer: Noodles → Ricotta → Sauce → Mozzarella. Repeat until full.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for another 20 minutes until the cheese bubbles.
