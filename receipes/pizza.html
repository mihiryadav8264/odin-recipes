Cake Page

Description

A crispy, golden crust topped with zesty tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and spicy pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade),
  • ½ cup pizza sauce,
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese,
  • pepperoni slices,
  • dried oregano,
  • cornmeal (for the pan).

Steps

  1. Heat: Preheat your oven to its highest setting (usually 475°F–500°F).
  2. Shape: Stretch the dough on a lightly floured surface into a circle.
  3. Assemble: Place dough on a pan sprinkled with cornmeal. Spread sauce thinly, leave a border for the crust, then add cheese and pepperoni.
  4. Bake: Slide it into the oven for 10-12 minutes until the edges are charred and the cheese is golden.
